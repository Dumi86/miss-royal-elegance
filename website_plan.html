# Website Plan for Miss Royal Elegance I've developed a design system called **"Imperial Grace"** inspired by the Miss Royal Elegance Instagram page. Here is a plan for the 4 core screens of the website: 1. **Home Screen:** A high-impact hero section with a background video or image from the pageant, an overview of the organization's mission, and links to current event information. 2. **Gallery & Past Winners:** A visually rich grid showcasing photos and videos from previous pageants, with dedicated sections for past titleholders. 3. **Application / Entry Page:** A clear, user-friendly page for aspiring contestants to learn about the requirements and submit their applications. 4. **Sponsors & Partners:** A professional layout to showcase the businesses and organizations supporting the pageant, which is crucial for building credibility and attracting new partners.
